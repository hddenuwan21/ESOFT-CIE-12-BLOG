    SriLankan cuisine

    Sri Lanka is one of the leading Asian countries. Many different ethnicities live in Sri Lanka.

    Sinhalese, Tamil, Muslim, Catholic are the main ones. Among them, there are foods that are unique to Sri Lanka.
    They vary according to different races, but mainly there are many foods that are eaten by all races. People in Asian countries often like their food to be spicy. It is special that many Asians like that taste.

    Among the foods unique to Sri Lanka, the main ones are rice and curries. In addition to this, locally grown jackfruit, pollos,nuts and yams are excellent.

    Sri Lankans did not like fast food in the past and it is not the case now. According to people's lifestyle, there is more tendency for fast food now.
