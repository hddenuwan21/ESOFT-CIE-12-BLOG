    THE MOVIE I LIKE MOST

    Today, movies have become an inseparable part of modern human society because of their ability to transport us to different worlds. It stirs emotions and captivates hearts and minds, prompting us to dream beyond our imaginations. As a powerful form of visual storytelling, movies also have the ability to influence our lives in general. From past to present, movies have evolved and adapted as artistic, entertaining and social.😴 To be honest, none of this matters to me😅.                            Beacuse of course I am a movie buff😏. There are plenty of movies that have left an indelible mark on my heart and mind💓.

    Among them, I still cannot forget the movie Extraction 1 and 2, which is in my heart. Both 1 and 2 are related stories.   The mission of our hero, the main character of this film, is to escape from another drag boss in Dhaka who has kidnapped a child of a drag boss in India😮. The mission in the second story is to save his dead wife's sister from a vicious mob😐.
