    small pizza easy make

    Materials needed


    * flour 200g
    * salt (tea spoon)5
    * boil chiken and sausage
    * big onion
    * capsicum
    * yeast (tea spoon) 3
    * suger(tea sppn)3
    * tomato 3
    * mozzarella chees
    * chilli

    Mathod of making


    Put in a bowl with yeast and sugar and keep for a while. Then add the mixture of water, cheese, yeast and sugar to the flour and knead for 10/15 minutes. Then let it sit for about 1.1/2 hours.


    After that, take the required amount and crush it into a circle with a roll. Then add onions, tomatoes, boiled meat or sausages and some chilies. Sprinkle some cheese powder on top. Then bake. Put together and bake.after can we eat
