    Let's Protect Our Environment

    Environment is our surrounding . It is a valuable gift of mother nature . It is the most important factor of living exitence . It Provides everything for humans, animals an other . Only the planet earth has such a live eco - system in the universe .

    The people do not protect our environment . They cut trees, kill animals, use polythenes, chemicals and pollute their surrounding . They have a bad habit of throwing eco - harmfull materials, gases, substances, fertilizers, industrial waste in to atmosphere . They buildup huge factories in big forests . The soil, air, water and animals are affected badly with environmental exploitations.

    There will be more bad effects due to pollutions such as unknown diseases, natural disasters, soil infertility, cancenrs, acidrains and etc. We most be eco - friendly.


    The polluted air will cause lung disenses, cancers even to animals . The plants, animals and other beings .
    We should reduce using those harmfull things . We should change in to natural sources such as solar power . We should reuse those plastic and polythene materials .

    The best way is to protect environment is recycling . This is our mother we must save her for future environment is the most valuable factor for all of us . We must practise to save it from our childhood .
