    The Country Life

    I like to live in the countryside. There are many reasons for my choice. The environment in the countryside is very clean. I can lead a healthy life in the village. The cost of living is less in the village than in the town. I can eat fresh fruits and vagetables and drink pure water. All most all the foodstuffs are available at a fair price. They are is no adulteration of food as in the towns. Villagers lead a simple life.

    Thery are shady trees and greeny fields in the village. These sights please my eyes. There are is no noise pollution here. There are is no air pollution too. Therefore i can breathe fresh air. The life in the country side is close to nature. The villagers are very friendly and helpful. They know one another well. Everyone likes to extend his or her coperation towards others in their needs. Heaps of garbage cannot be seen in the village.All the roads and by roads are clean.

    The only great disadvantage in a village is the lack of medical facilities. All so villages have only secondary schools. Children who need to continue their higher education have to go to the cities. There are temptations and evils such as liquor, drugs and smoking that can ruin young people in the cities. The village is less harmful.
    It is true that transport and educational facilities in the cities are better than in the villages. Anyway I prefer to live in the countryside.
